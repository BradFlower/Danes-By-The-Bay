Great Dane puppies are capable of eating adult dog chow, however some people prefer to use large breed puppy chow

Having rags or towels near water bowls and doors will help to lower the amount of slobber everywhere, just remember to wipe their jowls after letting them inside from playing. Tall dog gates are another option that can help section of parts of the House that are "off-limits" to your pup!

Large breed puppies need to have clear boundaries and expectations set from a young age, the earlier your pup understands this, the easier training should be. Socializing your Great Dane with other puppies, dogs, and people are crucial in developing the proper social skills to have a well mannered dog in any situation. Finally, make sure to limit amount of time your puppy is bored as this is the main time they get into Trouble, instead find something productive for them to do!
